Web Applications in Trend

Flat Ticket Rate For All

Posted: Thu 15 Jan, 2015
Tags: WAIT
Categories: General

WAIT 2015 launched with two ticket levels; the Early Bird and the Flat Rate. We've just made that a lot simpler.

We are busy planning, organising and arranging what will be an incredible conference. The very first Web Applications In Trend conference.

To celebrate we have dropped the Flat Rate ticket price from the original £195.00 value and have taken it all of the way down to match the Early Bird rate.

That's right. ALL attendees to WAIT 2015 will only have to pay the one single flat fee of £150.00 per ticket.

That's £150.00 for two days, two tracks of content per day.

Register your tickets today: http://waitconference.eu/#register

This will also make it easier for attendees to collectively level up and release more of the 'unlockable' items.

Tell your friends, tell your colleagues, tell your family, and we look forward to seeing you all in June.
