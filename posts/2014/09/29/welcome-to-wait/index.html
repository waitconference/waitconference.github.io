Web Applications in Trend

Welcome to WAIT

Posted: Mon 29 Sep, 2014
Tags: WAIT
Categories: General

On the list of 'pros' for those of us who create and mould the web is the great community that exists around our work. This community is a prime source of knowledge and support - for those who have been designing or developing for a while, or the newbies coming up the ranks.

One of the ways this sense of community is fostered, is through live events and conferences, which present us with opportunities to pick up on what others are doing, the new way of doing things and, for those who are that way inclined, the opportunity to teach and share.

A space opened up this year for a new event, one where this sense of community could be supported, while exposing folks to the latest and greatest that the web has to offer. That's when we set up Web Applications In Trend; where we hope to Educate, Inspire and Connect our delegates, and make it the must-not-miss conference of 2015.

Community is cornerstone to making this event a success, and we are looking forward to receiving your input on what you want to hear about and who you want to hear it from.

We look forward to this journey with you - and hope to see you next June!
